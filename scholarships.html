Available Scholarships

CSSS Scholarship

Financial support for meritorious Class 12 students from low-income families to pursue higher education.

Reliance Scholarship

Reliance Foundation awards scholarships on a merit-cum-means basis to undergraduate and postgraduate students.

PM YASASVI Scholarship

The PM YASASVI Scholarship is a government initiative that provides financial assistance to meritorious students

ONGC Scholarship

To provide financial support for higher education to meritorious and economically disadvantaged students from SC, ST, OBC, and General (EWS) categories.

Vidhyadhan Scholarship

Vidyadhan scholarship supports bright, economically challenged students with funding, mentoring, and career guidance for higher education after 10th grade.

NTSE Scholarship

The NTSE scholarship provides financial aid to Class 10 students for higher education, identifies and nurtures young talent, and enhances their academic profiles .

Colgate Scholarship

The Colgate Keep India Smiling Scholarship offers financial aid and mentorship to deserving students with financial needs.

SBI ASHA Scholarship

The SBI Asha Scholarship is a State Bank of India Foundation initiative providing financial aid to meritorious, low-income students
